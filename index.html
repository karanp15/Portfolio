Natural Simulations



The Natural Simulations course from Khan Academy really helped to open my eyes and show me just how significant the relationship between math and computer science is. We focused on noise, vectors, randomness and oscillations. The most interesting aspect of this course was the Creature projects, that I had to keep building on at the end of some of the lessons. For both mathematics and the sciences, the knowledge that I gained from this course will be extremely beneficial in the future.

Creative Coding Project



My Creative Coding project was based on the bubble sort algorithm. Bubble sort is a simple sorting algorithm that repeatedly goes through a list, swapping out items and does so until the list is sorted in the right order. There are other types of sorting algorithms but this is a simple one, to begin with. Using Dan Shiffimans youtube tutorials and website my partner and I were able to create a working visual interpretation of bubble sort.
